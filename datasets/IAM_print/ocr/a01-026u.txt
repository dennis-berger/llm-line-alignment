MR. LAIN MACLEOD, THE COLONIAI SECRETARY, DENILY IN THE COMMONS LAST MIGHT THAT
THERE HAVE BEEM SECRET REGOTATIONS ON NORTHERN RHODESIA'S FUTURE. THE NORTHERW
RHODESIA CONFERENCE IN LANDON HAS BEEN BOYOUTED BY THE TWO MAIN SETTLERS' PARTIES
- THE UNITED RECEIPANY AND THE DOMINION PARTY. BUT REPRESENTATIVES OF STR ROP
WELENSKY, PRIME MINISTER OF THE CENTRAL AFRICAN FEDERATION, WENT TO CHEGUES AT THE
WEEK-END FOR TALKS WITH MR. MACMILLAN.
