MR. LAIN MACLEOD, THE COLONIAI SECRETARY, DENIED IN THE COMMONS LAST MIGHT THAT
THERE HAVE BEEM SECRET REGOTATIONS ON NORTHERA RHODESIA'S FUTURE. THE NORTHERN
RHODESIA CONFERENCE IN LANDON HAS BEEN BOYOUTED BY THE TWO MAIN SETILES' PARTIES
- THE UNITED FEDERAL PARTY AND THE DOMINION PARTY. BUT REPRESENTATIVES OF STR ROP
WELENSKY, PRIME MINISTER OF THE CENTRAL AFRICAN FEDERATION, WENT TO CHEGUES AT THE
WEEK-END FOR TALKS WITH MR. MACMILLAN.
